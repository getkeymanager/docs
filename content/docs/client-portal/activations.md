## What Is This Page?

View and manage activations representing installations on your devices, machines, or domains. Deactivate installations you no longer use.

---

## When to Use This Page

Use this page to see where your licenses are activated, deactivate old installations, troubleshoot activation issues, or free up activation slots.

---

## What You Can Do Here

### Activation List

Shows license key, product, activation identifier (HWID/domain), activation date, last seen timestamp, status.

![Activation List Screenshot](placeholder-activations-activation-list.png)
*Screenshot placeholder - will be added*

### Activation Details

Identifier (HWID or domain), activation date, last seen, product version (if available), license key, product name, IP address (if tracked).

![Activation Details Screenshot](placeholder-activations-activation-details.png)
*Screenshot placeholder - will be added*

### Deactivation

Deactivate your own activations with confirmation. Immediately invalidates that installation, frees up activation slot, action is audited.

![Deactivation Screenshot](placeholder-activations-deactivation.png)
*Screenshot placeholder - will be added*

### Common Use Cases

Moving to a new device, reinstalling software, retiring old hardware, troubleshooting activation issues.

![Common Use Cases Screenshot](placeholder-activations-common-use-cases.png)

### Important

You cannot activate new devices from the portal. Activation happens through the product software itself.
