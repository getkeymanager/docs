## What Is This Page?

Manage all license keys in the system. View, create, assign, suspend, revoke, and monitor licenses throughout their lifecycle.

---

## When to Use This Page

Use this page to generate new licenses, assign licenses to customers, check license status, troubleshoot activation issues, or perform bulk operations.

---

## What You Can Do Here

### License List

View all licenses with advanced filtering, search, and bulk actions. Shows license key (masked), product, customer, status, expiry, and activation count.

![License List Screenshot](placeholder-licenses-license-list.png)
*Screenshot placeholder - will be added*

### License States

available, assigned, active, suspended, expired, revoked. State transitions are validated, audited, and cannot be skipped.

![License States Screenshot](placeholder-licenses-license-states.png)
*Screenshot placeholder - will be added*

### License Actions

View details, edit properties, assign to customer, suspend/unsuspend, revoke, deactivate activations, download offline license file, generate QR code.

![License Actions Screenshot](placeholder-licenses-license-actions.png)

### License Explainability

See why a license is invalid: failed rule, current state, expiry reason, HWID mismatch, activation limit details.
