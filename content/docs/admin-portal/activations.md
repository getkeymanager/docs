## What Is This Page?

Monitor and manage license activations representing real installations on devices, machines, or domains.

---

## When to Use This Page

Use this page to view all active installations, troubleshoot activation issues, deactivate specific installations, or monitor last-seen timestamps.

---

## What You Can Do Here

### Activation List

Shows license key, product, customer, identifier (HWID/domain), activation date, last seen timestamp, status, and IP address.

![Activation List Screenshot](placeholder-activations-activation-list.png)
*Screenshot placeholder - will be added*

### Activation Management

Filter by product/customer/license, search by identifier, deactivate individual activations, view activation details.

![Activation Management Screenshot](placeholder-activations-activation-management.png)
*Screenshot placeholder - will be added*

### Deactivation

Requires confirmation, fully audited, invalidates offline licenses, frees up activation slot.
