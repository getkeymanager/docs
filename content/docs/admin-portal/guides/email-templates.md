# Email Templates

This guide explains how to manage email templates in the Admin Portal.

## Overview

The Email Templates section allows you to create, edit, and manage templates for automated emails sent to customers and users.

## Key Features
- Create new email templates
- Edit template content and variables
- Activate or deactivate templates

## How to Use
1. Click 'Add Email Template' to create a new template.
2. Edit the content and insert dynamic variables as needed.
3. Activate or deactivate templates for use in automated workflows.
