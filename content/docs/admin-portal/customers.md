## What Is This Page?

Manage customer accounts that own licenses. View customer information, license ownership, and account status.

---

## When to Use This Page

Use this page to view customer details, check their license ownership, manage customer access, or review customer activity.

---

## What You Can Do Here

### Customer List

Shows customer name, email, total licenses, active licenses, registration date, and status. Supports search and filtering.
